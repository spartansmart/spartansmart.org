What Is Spartan SMART?

Spartan SMART is a community-based, faith-based released time Bible education outreach serving students in the Richmond Heights Local School District. With written parent permission, students are released during the school day to attend off-campus Bible instruction focused on faith development, character formation, and biblical understanding.
